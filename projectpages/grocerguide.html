Project Overview

There’s a disconnect between our digital grocery lists and the stores we shop in. Customers often don’t know which store has the best prices, where to find their items in-store, or if they’re overspending at checkout.

Grocerguide is a mobile app designed to help grocery shoppers save time and money by comparing prices before visiting the store and easily navigating the aisles for a more efficient shopping experience.

Understanding the User

I began by designing a survey targeted at a small, curated group within my network. Since I personally know these individuals and their grocery shopping habits, I opted to forego a preliminary screener survey. The following are the results of 11 unique participants:

  Survey Results

54.5% of participants were between the ages of 26 - 35.

100% of participants state that they use some form of list while grocery shopping.

100% of participants frequent 2 or more grocery stores in order to complete their list.
